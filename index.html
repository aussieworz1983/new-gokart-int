All About Us

For over ten years, Go Kart International has been providing the ultimate go-karting experience at Fantasy Island Themed Resort. We're passionate about our job and our customers, which is why we work hard to ensure that every visitor has an unforgettable experience.

We're open 7 days a week, from March till November, offering you plenty of opportunities to experience the thrill of go-karting. Our prices offer great value for money, making our go-karting experience accessible for all ages and budgets.

Whether you're a thrill-seeker or just looking for a fun family activity, Go Kart International has something for everyone. Our high-octane rides are designed to provide maximum excitement and enjoyment, while our focus on safety ensures that you can have peace of mind while you race.

At Go Kart International, we believe that go-karting should be a fun and accessible activity for everyone. That's why we offer a range of rides that cater to all levels of experience. From our beginner-friendly karts to our high-performance rides, we guarantee that you'll have a blast on our tracks.

So, what are you waiting for? Come and experience the thrill of go-karting at Go Kart International, where fun and excitement never stop!
